Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using CamConnect

What is CamConnect?

CamConnect is a modern video chat platform that connects you with random people from around the world. Our platform uses WebRTC technology to provide high-quality, real-time video conversations without any downloads or installations required.

Is CamConnect free to use?

Yes! CamConnect offers free basic video chat with no hidden fees. We also offer premium memberships with additional features like gender filtering, ad-free experience, and priority matching. The choice is yours - use it free or upgrade for enhanced features.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is required for basic video chat. You can start chatting immediately as a guest. However, creating a free account unlocks additional features like saving your preferences, accessing chat history, and using gender filters (with premium membership).

How does the matching system work?

Our intelligent matching system pairs you with other users who are currently online and looking to chat. Premium members can filter by gender preference, while free users are matched randomly. Simply click "Next" to move to a new conversation partner at any time.

Is my privacy protected?

Absolutely. We take privacy seriously and never share your personal information with third parties. Guest users remain completely anonymous, and registered users can choose how much information to share. All video streams are encrypted and not recorded.

Can I use CamConnect on my phone?

Yes! CamConnect is fully responsive and works on all devices - desktop computers, laptops, tablets, and smartphones. Our platform is optimized for both iOS and Android devices, so you can chat on the go.

What are the community guidelines?

We maintain a respectful community. All users must be 18 years or older. Harassment, hate speech, illegal activities, and non-consensual behavior are strictly prohibited. Users who violate our guidelines may be banned from the platform.

How do I report inappropriate behavior?

If you encounter someone violating our community guidelines, click the report button during your conversation or use our contact form to submit a report. Our moderation team reviews all reports and takes appropriate action to maintain a safe environment.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and PayPal. All payments are processed securely through Stripe, and we never store your payment information on our servers.

Can I cancel my premium membership?

Yes, you can cancel your premium membership at any time through your account settings. Your premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period. There are no cancellation fees or long-term commitments.
